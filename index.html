Hui Zeng

I did my PhD at Hong Kong Polytechnic University, where I was advised by Prof. Lei Zhang. I did my BSc and MSc at Dalian University of Technology. I had worked as a research intern at DJI, and DAMO Academy (Alibaba Group).

Email /  GitHub /  Google Scholar / 

Research

I am interested in Image Processing, Computational Photography and Computer Vision. Much of my research is about deep learning based image restoration and enhancement.
